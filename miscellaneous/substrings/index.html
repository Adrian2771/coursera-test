Dealing with substrings

Enter a string of characters in the field below. When click "Action", the longest sequence in alphabetical order will be displayed. If there's more than one such substring (sequence), only the first will be displayed.

Click on "Predefined" and a collection of predefined strings will be passed in to the app. The longest substring in alphabetical order will be displayed for each string of the collection.

Click on "Substrings" to display all the substrings of the provided string.
